Collaborative Learning Discussion Two

Reflections From This Discussion

Assignment

This discussion asks the participants about the criticism of authors towards the characteristics of CVSS, while providing their own opinion on this topic. There are several alternatives to CVSS, so a specific alternative must be chosen with a justification for why it should be used.

Discussion Outcomes

The primary source of the criticism towards CVSS seems to be the fact, that while it claims to be considered a quantitative assessment, since the importance of each metric relies on the business using the model, this makes the model subjective, rather than objective. Therefore, CVSS leans more towards the qualitative risk assessment. Other discussions talked about CVSS not being efficient enough to deal with cyber risk quantification, and instead referred to other frameworks such as SSVC.

While there might be a variety of different frameworks available, SSVC seems to be prevelant throughout the discussion, as it acts as a potential replacement for CVSS, while being more customizable, and uses a modular decision-making approach, which uses a quantitative modelling methods for determining future outcomes based on previous events. Other frameworks such as VPR were also discussed as a potential alternative due to its simplicity compared to CVSS, while providing additional machine learning models that work together to forecast threats.
