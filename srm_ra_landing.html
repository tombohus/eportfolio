Summary of the Project

Consisting of the Introduction, Risk Assessment talking about techniques such as OpenFAIR and OCTAVE, which consists of three parts: Building Asset-Based Threat Profiles, Identifying Infrastructure Vulnerabilities and Developing Security Strategy and Plans.

This is then followed by a risk assessment for the potential digitalisation, including any proposed changes for the digital transformation.

The document keeps addressing any risk and threat modelling attempts using STRIDE, OCTAVE and PASTA.

The document is completed by covering any potential mitigations, including procedural controls, technical controls as well as physical controls to improve different aspects of security.

This is followed by a list of recommendations around these mitigations.
