Collaborative Learning Discussion One

Reflections From This Discussion

Assignment

The assignment asks the participants about their view on the term "Industry 4.0" as well as asking for real-world examples, supplemented by supporting or contradicting literature

Discussion Outcomes

There were many interesting posts being discussed, but the popular answers to the above questions were:

Industry 4.0 can be defined as a host of new technologies triggering economic and social change, followed by smart factory applications being used for digitalizing supply chain management, as well as the requirements for employees' technical and soft skills. Other valid answers were a transformation and internationalisation of the industry, advancements in modern technology, such as cloud computing and internet of things.

Some of the popular examples of risks associated with business include financial risks and data security risks. Financial risks could originate from lack of budget for advanced technology and software, while data risks could include a lack of training for employees and staffing issues. Additional factors such as lack of skills are also relevant

While there is an availability of both supporting and contradicting journals, certain studies suggest that there are four possible solutions, such as an immigration for skilled labor, offshore HR outsourcing, global PEO and umbrella company.
